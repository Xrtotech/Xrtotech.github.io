# Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. This application is designed with a data-minimization approach and does **not** collect or store any customer personal information.

## Information We Do **Not** Collect

We **do not** collect, store, or access the following data:

- Personal information of your customers (e.g., name, email, address)  
- Payment or billing information  
- Order details or transaction records  

## Information We Collect

To provide the core functionality of this application, we only store the following data:

- Product names (used solely for display and event configuration)  
- Event start and end times (used to manage the timing of sales events)  

This data is used strictly within the scope of the app’s intended functionality. We do **not** use it for analytics, marketing, or share it with any third parties.

## Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable security measures to protect the limited data we store from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

## Third-Party Services

This application does **not** share your store’s data or your customers’ data with any third-party service providers.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the application after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
